There are 4 basic principles and they are repetition, proximity, alignment, and contrast.

Contrast is to avoid elements on the page that are merely similar. Contrast was used on my website by having many different colours and having all the colours works together.

Repetition is to repeat visual elements of the design throughout the piece. Repetition was used by having headings be the same colour and the links all be the same colour.

Alignment is where every element has some visual connection with another element of the page. Alignment was used at the bottom where everything is in a relatively straight line.

Proximity is where items relating to each other should be grouped close together. Proximity was used when the relative links and the interesting links were grouped up separately.
